Sean Tambling
5.0 out of 5 starsIm sold on this robot!!
Reviewed in the United States on June 21, 2023
I dont write many reviews. Most things we buy meet our expectations. This robot blew my expectations away.

Ordering/shipping:
The first one arrived damaged badly. Despite being well packaged, it looked like something very heavy with a sharp corner had been dropped on the box and it broke the base station, and the robot though it was in an additional box. I didnt like that I was shipped a box that was very obviously damaged before it was shipped. I contacted Amazon, and they had me ship it back at their expense and sent me another one. A bit of a hassle but Amazon made it worth my while with a small token of a refund.

Overall experience:
Ive had this robot for about 1.5 months. WE love it. We have a 9 month old golden retriever (who we dearly love, named Elvis, yes, hes a rock star) and as everyone knows they shed like a blizzard dropping snow at the North Pole in the dead of winter. Every day the hair!! At first I only used the robot down stairs and just let it do its default cleaning, which is to vacuum and mop all at the same time. Wow!! What a huge difference this robot has made on the cleanliness of our floors and enriched our lives by not having to sweep and mop every day from the torrent of hair. We have hardwood floors downstairs and we use a mix of Bona hardwood floor cleaner with water in the clean water tank. The hardwood looks absolutely amazing each time the robot runs.
After 2 weeks I wanted to see how good the robot would work on the carpet upstairs. I will say this was the first challenge. Anything made in China is going to have a lack of instructions and you kind of have to experiment a little and figure out how to make it map a second floor and vacuum the carpet. The app for the robot is great. Great maps, good functionality. Some of the instructions in the app could be better for taking the robot from one floor to the next and transitioning from hardwoods to carpet. The very first time I took it upstairs, I set it down, followed the instructions in the app to put it in the room you want to start a new map for and I pressed play to start the mapping / cleaning process. This works fine for the first floor/map. It might work for some people, but didnt for me. I was short on time this day and gave up and put the robot back on base station. A few days later I tried vacuuming the upstairs again. This time I had taken the mop pads off to clean separately (really no need to) and so I changed the cleaning mode from dual cleaning (vacuum/mop) to only vacuum. I let it map the second floor and then had it vacuum. Again, its mapping was great. I recognized the stairs and pulled back and found all of the rooms and closet perfectly. The VACUUMING!! It stripes your carpet like a champ! It does a great job imho. About a week later I took the robot back upstairs to vacuum again and this time with the mop pads to clean the mop the bathroom floors. Again I ran into trouble. I assumed I could just press play and it would recognize the carpet and vacuum the carpet and in the bathrooms vacuum/mop like downstairs. Instead it would start (it goes through a routine where it discovers which room it is in) and start to vacuum and then it would want to return to the base station. This time I played with it, and suffice to say after lots of playing with it, the trick is to use the shortcut in the app to VACUUM and then MOP. This works every time. Overall WE love this system!

The robot:
Its well designed and not cheap but not overly heavy either. The drive wheels are large. The components are well engineered. The roller bar is easily removed and cleaned of hair. A cleaning tool is provided in the box for cutting the hair from the roller bar. The roller bar isnt heavy duty by any means. I will probably need to replace it before too long. On one of the cleaning sessions I left a small usb cable on the floor. It got sucked up and the robot didnt recognize it quickly and the roller was slightly damaged. The robot did stop and didnt make a big mess. Would have preferred it to recognize the cord and not clean over it. The robot recognizes toys and furniture well. It has one rotation sweeper bush and is easily removed to clean hair from it. The filter is easily removed and cleaned. The cleaning pads are held in place magnetically and easily removed to either clean or replace the pads which are held on with very strong velcro. Its vacuum is very strong and when cleaning the downstatirs hardwood floors and transitions over randomly placed throw rugs, it immediately recognizes the different surface and increases the vacuum power and raises the mop pads while on rugs and then reduces the vacuum and lowers the mop pads again when transitioning back to hardwood floors. The robot has 3 buttons. One that will have the robot begin cleaning, one to have the robot return to the base station, and one to have the robot spot clean an area it is set down in. From the app you can manage the volume of water used when cleaning, and the suction power when vacuuming.

The base station:
The vacuum station does an excellent job of removing the vacuum collected debris from the robot into a bag and comes with an extra bag. The bag is easily removed. The clean water and the dirty water tanks are easily identified and not confusable. I add a 1/3 of a tank of Bona hardwood cleaner to the clean water tank each refill. The dirty water tank is easy to clean. When the robot returns to the base station it, the base station removes the debris from the robot and then starts cleaning the mop pads. This is accomplished by rotating the pads over a removable surface that scrubs the pads well and the bottom of the base station is flooded with cleaning solution and after the cleaning cycle it sucks the dirty water through the drain into the dirty water tank. The system comes with a cleaning brush and a cleaning mode to flood the base with the robot removed and you use the brush to scrub the debris free and it is sucked into the dirty water tank afterwards.

The app:
Overall the app is great. The maps are detailed and zoomable. You can modify the map by merging rooms, renaming them and the different maps. No clean zones can be created. Schedules can be created. Specific tasks can be created.

Overall we love this system. Buy it!

Turbo97se
3.0 out of 5 starsVery nice vacuum but a lot of software bugs or improvements needed to make this great
Reviewed in the United States on July 28, 2023
Likes:
1. Like the relatively easy set up
2. Looks decent
3. moping seems pretty good
4. Can be integrated with Home Assistant and therefore controlled by Siri with a bridge. Love that this function is supported even if it is not official.
5. Versatile - if things (furniture shoes etc) move around, the vacuum mop can adapt.

Dislikes:
1. The user interface is not easy to use
2. once an area is mapped, you cannot easily add a room or area or edit current map, you need to remap the whole area again
3. The Wifi is really pretty weak and the UI to change the Wifi access point is very painful. You basically have to reset the unit just to connect to another access point .. .ridiculous! It does not tell you if Wifi is weak and I tried to update the firmware several times before realizing it kept failing due to weak wifi signal. I then connected it to my Mesh wifi which has an access point nearby (literally right next door to it) and it worked .. however it does not switch automatically to other Wifi access points (even my mesh network) it just latches on to one AP and sticks to it no matter what and loses signal regularly .. fortunately it has the smarts to go home without a wifi signal and can then recover
4. The mop is both a good and poor design at the same time. The mop rotates vs being dragged around and so leaves what it touches cleaner. However, for whatever reason, they decided to put them at the back of the unit so it cannot mop the skirting boards at all and so there is a gap of maybe 3-4 inches at the perimeter that it cannot mop at all. This is a major design miss in my mind.
5. Wish the water tanks were bigger. They need filling/emptying very regularly.
6. The SW keeps defaulting back to vacuum mode only for some reason so I always have to check that it mops and vacuums when I have set it to cleaning mode. Often have to set it to mop and vacuum mode.
